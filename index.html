For grown-ups 🧑‍🤝‍🧑

Bible Buddies is a narrated tap-and-learn Bible game for ages 2–6: a tappable bookshelf of 89 books (each with a one-line summary, a hero, and a lesson), counting games, hero and which-book quizzes, heart-lesson matching, and 8 read-aloud stories with bouncing words. Every prompt is read out loud, so pre-readers can play on their own.

Progress saves on this device automatically — each child picks their animal and picks up where they left off. Levels adjust up and down on their own as your child answers.

About the 89 books: this shelf merges the Hebrew Bible (Tanakh), the Apocrypha / Deuterocanonical books, and the Ethiopian Orthodox Tewahedo broader canon — traditionally counted as 81 books, the largest Bible canon in the world. Counting every named book separately (traditions group some together), the merged shelf holds 89. Summaries are simplified and kept gentle for little ones.

Sacred & Hebrew names: the game says Yah and Yehoshua HaMashiach, and uses Hebrew names for people — Moshe, Noach, Dovid, Ester, Yonah, Yehudit, Chanoch… As children advance, the English names are taught alongside: at level 3 in Bible Heroes the cards show both (Moshe · Moses), and the bookshelf read-alouds add "in English, Moses." Book titles stay in English so pairs like Yonah-in-the-book-of-Jonah teach the connection naturally.
